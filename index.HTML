About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelike game where your goal is simple: survive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. As you defeat enemies, you'll gain experience, level up, and unlock powerful new abilities and weapons.

Inspired by popular roguelike survival games, Monster Survivors combines strategic gameplay with fast-paced action. Every run is different with procedurally generated maps and randomized power-ups, ensuring a unique experience every time you play.

With its addictive gameplay loop and progressive difficulty, Monster Survivors will test your reflexes and strategic thinking as you fight to survive just a little bit longer with each attempt.

Game Features

Endless Survival Gameplay

Face waves of increasingly difficult monsters and see how long you can survive in this challenging roguelike experience.

Character Progression

Level up your character, unlock new abilities, and discover powerful weapon combinations to overcome the monster hordes.

Diverse Enemy Types

Battle against various monster types, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns that will keep you on your toes.

Randomly Generated Maps

Every playthrough features procedurally generated environments, ensuring a fresh challenge every time you play.

Survival Tips

Keep Moving

Standing still is a death sentence. Always stay on the move to avoid being surrounded by monsters.

Prioritize Upgrades

When leveling up, focus on synergistic abilities that complement your playstyle and weapon choices.

Collect Experience Gems

Make sure to collect all experience gems dropped by defeated monsters to level up faster and gain an edge.

Watch the Clock

Be prepared for difficulty spikes at certain time intervals. Tougher monsters will appear as you progress.
